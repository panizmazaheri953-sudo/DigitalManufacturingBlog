Production Platform Approved

Based on FESTO CP Lab verification, SPC dimensional analysis, and 5 physical print trials, the Bambu P1S is hereby approved as the sole production platform for the Phones-R-Us casing contract.

All quality specifications have been met. The production line configuration — 25 Bambu P1S printers, weekdays 24/7 and weekends 8h/day — is validated to deliver 50,000 interchangeable casing pairs per year at the required dimensional tolerances.

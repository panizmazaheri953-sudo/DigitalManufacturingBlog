01 — Client Requirement

The Challenge

Bottom Casing

Drawing provided by client — must match exactly ±0.1mm tolerance.

Top Casing

Reverse engineer from physical part — drawing had missing dimensions and confusing cross-sections.

Interchangeability

Must fit with existing master casings from another supplier.

Assembly Line

Must flow through FESTO CP Lab without jamming — all 6 stations.

Production Target

50,000 complete phones per year (50,000 top + 50,000 bottom casings).

Timeline

9 weeks to present sample for testing and validation.

02 — Timeline

9-Week Journey

Week 1-2
Ultimaker trials — 15 prints, 0 passed FESTO measurement station.
Week 3-4
Failure analysis — identified laser measurement issue (1.18mm vs 1.6-2.2mm spec).
Week 5-6
Switched to Bambu P1S — first prints: 80% success rate, 3 perfect.
Week 7-8
FESTO validation — Bambu prints pass all 6 stations.
Week 9
Final delivery — 50,000/year plan: 25 printers running 24/7.

ENCLOS3D Manufacturing Co.
Manchester, UK

Phones-R-Us
Challenge.

Paniz Manufacturing was tasked with delivering a complete digital manufacturing solution for Phones-R-Us — a client requiring 50,000 interchangeable phone casings per year, across both top casing and bottom casing.

The brief demanded not just volume, but dimensional precision, full traceability, FESTO CP Lab verification, and a repeatable production workflow — from CAD modelling through to final assembly verification and full simulation, all within a 9-week prototype delivery window.

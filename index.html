🎯 50,000 Phones / Year

Phones-R-Us requires 50,000 complete phones per year (50,000 top casings + 50,000 bottom casings). This target was achieved with 25 Bambu P1S printers running 24/7 at 80% success rate.

Calculation: 50,000 ÷ 260 days = 192 phones/day → with 80% success: 240 prints/day × 2.5 hours = 600 hours/day ÷ 24 hours = 25 printers.

📅 9-Week Prototype Delivery

Week 1-2: Ultimaker trials — 15 prints, 0 passed
Week 3-4: Failure analysis — identified laser measurement issue
Week 5-6: Switched to Bambu P1S — 80% success rate
Week 7-8: FESTO validation — Bambu passes all stations
Week 9: Final delivery — 25 printer solution

✅ 80% Success Rate

Based on 5 test prints with the Bambu Lab P1S: 4 successful prints, 3 were "perfect" quality. 1 failure was due to USB file interruption (not printer hardware).

Ultimaker comparison: 15 prints, 0 passed FESTO measurement station — 0% success rate.

⏱️ 2.5 Hours Print Time

Time to print BOTH casings together on Bambu P1S at good quality settings (0.15mm layer height, 25% infill). Super high quality takes 4 hours.

Normal quality (0.2mm layer) takes 2 hours, but 0.15mm was selected for optimal dimensional accuracy.

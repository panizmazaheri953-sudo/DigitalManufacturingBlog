01
Paniz Manufacturing Co.
Manchester, UK

Phones-R-Us
Challenge.

Paniz Manufacturing was tasked with delivering a complete digital manufacturing solution for Phones-R-Us — a client requiring 50,000 interchangeable phone casings per year, across both a reverse-engineered top casing and a technically-specified bottom casing.

The brief demanded not just volume, but dimensional precision, full traceability, and a repeatable production workflow — from CAD modelling through to final FESTO CP Lab assembly verification, all within a 9-week prototype delivery window.
